Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

GameBox - Fun Games ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed by Saraschandra Ajjarapu. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. GameBox is a fully offline game collection that does not require an internet connection, user accounts, or any form of registration.

Data Storage

All game data (scores, preferences, and game progress) is stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit, store, or process any data on external servers.

Third-Party Services

GameBox does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

GameBox is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users. Since the app works entirely offline with no data collection, it is safe for children to use.

Permissions

GameBox does not request any device permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, or any other device features.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
